The conference will take place in Sun Yat-sen Kaifeng Hotel which is a four-star standard boutique hotel of 296 guest rooms. It locates within the university campus that hosts many historic architectures and artifacts (University map). It is also on the Pearl River bank with most of its guestrooms enjoying a panoramic view of the city’s modern presentation.
