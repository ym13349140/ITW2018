Guangzhou ←→ Hong Kong

Guangzhou is 120km northwest of Hong Kong. You may come to Guangzhou by transiting in Hong Kong. From Hong Kong, you can take an Express Train from Hung Hum Railway Station to Guangzhou East Train Station. The Express Train runs every 50 mins and the journey takes 2 hours. You can also take the Express Train for your return to Hong Kong. Alternatively, you can also take a coach which will take three hours.
