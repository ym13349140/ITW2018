Guangzhou to Hong Kong

There are 3 main ways from Guangzhou to Hong Kong,they are bus,train and plane.

By Bus

In these three ways,taking a bus is the most convenient.You can take a bus from Guangzhou directly to Hong Kong Kowloon.The whole trip takes about three hours.You can get on the bus at the following places:

Hua Hai Building,Guangdong Hotel,Guangzhou Hotel,Fangcun Tea House,Guangdong Geological Landscape Hotel,Jincheng Hotel,President Hotel and Yutong Hotel.
