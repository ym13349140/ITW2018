Registration

Registration will open by September.

Registration fee includes:

  • Admission to all sections;
  • Coffee and cake during section breaks;
  • Lunch from November 26 to 29;
  • Welcome reception on November 25;
  • Pearl River night cruise on November 26;
  • Banquet at Sky No.1 Restaurant on November 27;
  • Excursion to Baiyun Mountain on November 28.

Each accepted and invited paper must have at least one author registered by Sept 27, 2018 11:59pm Hawaii Time (UTC-10). Accepted papers that have no registered author by that date will not appear in the final program nor the proceedings. Up to 3 papers may be covered by a single full registration. A student registration (reduced fee) covers a single paper.
