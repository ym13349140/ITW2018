Tutorial Call

Two parallel tutorials will be organized in the afternoon of Nov. 25th. Each will last for 3 hours including a half an hour coffee break. We call for tutorials that represent state-of-the-art research in information and coding theory and their emerging disciplines. Tutorial proposals should be written within 2 pages, highlighting the title, abstract, impact and outline of contents. The proposals should be submitted together with a CV of the presenter(s).

Please submit your proposal to our tutorial chairs:

  • Krishna Narayanan, krn@tamu.edu
  • Pascal Vontobel, pascal.vontobel@gmail.com
The submission deadline is Mar. 1, 2018 and the acceptance will be announced on Mar. 15, 2018.

All participant of ITW 2018 are entitled attend the tutorial without additional charge, but reservation will be needed to contain the number of attendees within the venue capacity.
