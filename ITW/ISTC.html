ISTC 2018

The International Symposium on Turbo Codes & Iterative Information Processing (ISTC) 2018 will take place in Hong Kong from December 3 – 7, the week after ITW 2018. Hong Kong is only 120 km southeast of Guangzhou with a train journey of 2 hours. For travel information, you can see in our Travel page.
