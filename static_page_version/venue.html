The conference will take place in Sun Yat-sen Kaifeng Hotel which is a four-star standard boutique hotel of 296 guestrooms. It locates within the university campus that hosts many historic architectures and artifacts (University map). It is also on the Pearl River bank with most of its guestrooms enjoying a nice view of the city’s modern presentation.

From Baiyun International Airport

1) By Taxi: cost ~ 200 RMB, time ~ 50 mins, show the driver

2) By Airport Express Shuttle: cost ~ 20 RMB, time ~ 1 hr. Line 6 (To New Pearl River International Hotel)

3) By Metro: cost 12 RMB, time ~ 1 hr 30 mins

Note that Sun Yat-sen University metro station is 2km away from the venue. You are recommended to further take a taxi from the station.


From Guangzhou East Train Station

1) By Taxi: cost ~ 40 RMB, time ~ 25 mins, show the driver

2) By Metro: cost 4 RMB, time ~ 23 mins

Note that Sun Yat-sen University metro station is 2km away from the venue. You are recommended to further take a taxi from the station.
